Diabetes

Diet Plan

  • Breakfast: Oats porridge with ghee and banana
  • Mid-morning: Green tea + almonds
  • Lunch: Steamed vegetables, brown rice, moong dal
  • Evening Snack: Apple or cucumber slices
  • Dinner: Khichdi or light vegetable soup

Ayurvedic Recommendations

  • Include bitter and astringent foods.
  • Avoid sugar, fried, and oily foods.
  • Drink warm water with turmeric daily.
